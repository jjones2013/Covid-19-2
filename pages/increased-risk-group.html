Watch for symptoms*

Reported illnesses have ranged from mild symptoms to severe illness and death for confirmed coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID–19) cases.

The following symptoms may appear 2–14 days after exposure.**

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
