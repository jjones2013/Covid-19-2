Watch for symptoms*

Reported illnesses have ranged from mild symptoms to severe illness and death for confirmed coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID–19) cases.

The following symptoms may appear 2–14 days after exposure.**

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath

*Much of the information on this website is based on information obtained from the website Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

**This is based on what has been seen as the incubation period of MERS–CoV virus.

If you develop emergency warning signs for COVID–19, get medical attention immediately. Emergency warning signs include*:

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • New confusion or inability to arouse
  • Bluish lips or face

*The list is not all inclusive. Please consult your medical provider for any other symptoms that are severe or concerning.
